Frequently
asked questions.

Is FundFlow right for me?
FundFlow is perfect for freelancers, remote workers, and businesses who deal with international clients and currencies. We simplify the chaos of borderless finance.
How do I set it up?
Simply download the app or sign up on web. Verify your identity with a photo ID, and you'll be ready to create accounts in 30+ currencies instantly.
What are the fees?
We charge zero monthly fees for personal accounts. Transfers are charged at the real exchange rate plus a small 0.5% fee. No hidden costs, ever.
Is my money safe?
Yes. We use 256-bit encryption and are fully regulated. Your funds are safeguarded in dedicated accounts with tier-1 banks, separate from our own funds.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. There are no lock-in contracts. You can close your account instantly from the settings menu if you decide to leave.
